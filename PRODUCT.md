## Design Context

### Users
**Target Audience**: Tech enthusiasts looking for curated studio gear and workspaces.
**Context**: Desktop-heavy browsing, likely looking for aesthetic and functional inspiration.
**Job to be Done**: Explore and discover high-quality, "essential" technology gear in a distraction-free environment.

### Brand Personality
**Voice & Tone**: Minimal, Precise, Organic.
- **Minimal**: Removing noise to let the products speak.
- **Precise**: Sharp alignments, intentional use of space, and refined typography.
- **Organic**: Using natural tones (Bone, Sage, Moss) and smooth transitions to soften the technical nature of the products.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: Refined Minimalism (Lumina).
- **Theme**: Light (Soft Bone) as primary, Dark (Obsidian Mist) as secondary.
- **Key Elements**: Baskervville serif for editorial headings, high-contrast modular type scale, and subtle OKLCH color shifts.

### Design Principles
1. **Curated Focus**: Every element on the screen should have a purpose. If it doesn't add value to the discovery experience, remove it.
2. **Textural Harmony**: Blend the technical (precise grids) with the organic (soft colors and smooth motion).
3. **Intentional Transitions**: Use motion to guide the eye between states, avoiding abrupt changes.
4. **Accessible Precision**: High-tech doesn't mean low accessibility; ensure all interactive elements are clearly labeled and keyboard-navigable.
